FAQ

Monday - Friday: 9am - 5pm

Call us on 01 555 1234 or email info@thecroatianfoodshop.ie

There is no minimum spend per order.

We accept all major debit and credit cards.

To place an order, click on an item and press ‘add to cart’. Once you have finished shopping, select ‘go to secure checkout’.
If you already have an account, please log in to complete your order. If you don’t have an account, don’t worry, you can still complete your order by filling in the form on the left of your screen.

To open an account, click on ‘my account’ and ‘register’. Enter your email address and create a username and password. Once you’ve done this, you will then receive an email to ‘verify’ your account. You are now ready to start shopping.

To sign in, click on ‘my account’ and ‘login’. Enter your username and password and click ‘sign in’.

If you can’t remember your password, click ‘forgot your password’ and you will be prompted to enter your email address to ‘reset your password’.

When logged in to your account, click on ‘my profile’, your order history will appear on the right of the page.

You can update your user information at any time by logging into your account and clicking on ‘my profile’. You can change your phone number and address here. We do not store your card details.
